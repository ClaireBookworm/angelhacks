AngelHacks

A 12-hour middle school and high school hackathon working to give back to the Los Angeles community

AngelHacks strives to empower students to make a difference by
working together to solve LA's most pressing problems.

About AngelHacks

AngelHacks is a 12-hour hackathon devoted to giving students the best experience ever and encourage them to learn computer science in school and otherwise!

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this cost anything? 💰

Nothing! AngelHacks is 100% free, and we provide meals, snacks, swags, workshops, and other perks!

Can I Come? 💻

Any middle school or high school student is allowed to participate! And even if you're not in that age range, we're open to mentors or younger students, so please email us at hello@angelhacks.org!

What if I DON'T know how to code? 👶

The goal of AngelHacks is to encourage computer science education, so, if you're a complete beginner, that's fine and dandy! We'll have mentors and workshops for you to learn the tools you'll need for you project.

What if I DO know how to code? 👶

Are you an expert? If so, come! A hackathon is still a competition and you can earn prizes for creating amazing apps and programs that help the community! Our prizes are really awesome...

Wait, Hacking, that's illegal, isn't it?

"Hacking" doesn't mean the same thing that it did 10 years ago, and is being redefined by the young generation of makers. In this case, we're still hacking, but we're hacking the systme through making new, innovative products rather than typing fanatically on a computer in homebrew terminal mode and looking very sci-fi like.
