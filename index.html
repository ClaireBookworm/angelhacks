About Us

Who are we? AngelHacks is a free, 12 hour coding competition for any high schooler who wants to attend! AngelHacks strives to empower students to make a difference by working together to solve LA's most pressing problems. In this short period of time, you will discover new friends and form a team, code an awesome project, and walk away with a ton of swag and prizes.

Founded by Claire Wang, our hackathon aims to inspire the community to engage in computer science together. The focus of this event will be making a difference on the community through programming and innovative projects. Don't know how to code? Attend a workshop run by an expert from both our sponsors and the local area.
